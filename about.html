WHO WE ARE

Industrial Solutions Built Around
Protection, Precision and Practical Use

Packion Systems & Polyfoam Pvt Ltd is an industrial solutions company based in Pune, Maharashtra. We provide custom foam packaging, reusable dunnage, antistatic foam solutions and spray technology support for automotive, electronics, tooling and manufacturing applications.

Our focus is to help businesses protect their components, improve handling efficiency and support reliable industrial processes through practical, application-based solutions.
